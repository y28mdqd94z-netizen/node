# Engineering Principles (Short-Form)

Rules:
- Correctness over convenience.
- Explicit contracts at boundaries.
- Favor simplicity and cohesion over abstraction.
- Design for testability and observability.

Checks:
- Interfaces are documented and validated.
- Failure modes are explicit.
